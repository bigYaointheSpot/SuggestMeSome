Consumer Health Data Notice

Notice covering local workout, readiness, recovery, coaching, Premium Unlock, support, and optional Apple Health-derived information that may reveal health status.

Version

Version 2.3. Effective 2026-05-02.

Support: support@suggestmesome.app. Privacy: privacy@suggestmesome.app.

Apple Health-derived recovery data stays on device in this release and is not sent to CloudKit or a sync backend.

SuggestMeSome does not use Apple Health data for advertising and does not include ads or third-party analytics in this public launch baseline.

Premium Unlock is a one-time in-app purchase. Manual workout logging remains free.

Production cloud accounts, Sign in with Apple, backend sync, coach collaboration, private sharing, and push notifications are not included in this v1 App Store release.

Scope

SuggestMeSome processes workout, readiness, recovery, and coaching information that may reveal health status. This notice is written for a U.S. release that includes Washington residents and other state consumer-health privacy requirements.

Categories and sources

  • Workout history and exercise performance
  • Readiness check-ins, daily coach records, and training-recovery summaries
  • Coaching, fatigue, adaptation, and progress outputs that may reveal health-related inferences
  • Optional Apple Health data you authorize
  • Local backup or export files you choose to create
  • Support records you choose to send that involve consumer health data

Consumer health data may come from the data you manually enter, workouts you log, optional Apple Health data you authorize, and support or privacy messages you choose to send.

Purposes and sharing

SuggestMeSome uses this information to provide the workout logging, coaching, recovery, analytics, optional Apple Health, Apple Watch, widgets, Live Activity, Premium Unlock, and support features you request.

This v1 release keeps consumer health data local by default and does not enable production cloud sync or collaboration sharing.

Apple Health-derived recovery data stays on device in this release and is not sent to a sync backend.

SuggestMeSome does not use Apple Health data for advertising and does not include ads or third-party analytics in this public launch baseline.

Your rights

  • Confirm whether SuggestMeSome is processing your consumer health data
  • Request access to or export of consumer health data, including categories of recipients where required by law
  • Request deletion of consumer health data
  • Withdraw consent for future collection or sharing where consent is the legal basis
  • Appeal a denied privacy request where applicable law requires it

Use the in-app local export, backup, and delete controls, the privacy choices page, or contact privacy@suggestmesome.app.

Important limits

Deleting local app data does not delete records stored in Apple Health. Production cloud sync, coach collaboration, private sharing, and push notifications are not included in this v1 App Store release.
