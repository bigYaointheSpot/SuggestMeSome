Eligibility and product scope

SuggestMeSome is intended for adults age 18 and older. The v1 app provides workout logging, analytics, smart workout and program suggestions, optional Apple Health integration, Apple Watch support, widgets, Live Activity support, and wellness-oriented coaching support.

No medical use

SuggestMeSome provides fitness and wellness guidance only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it should not be used for emergency or medical decisions.

Check with a doctor before making medical decisions, changing care, or relying on recovery or readiness results after illness, injury, pain, pregnancy, or other health concerns.

Premium Unlock

Premium Unlock is a one-time in-app purchase. It unlocks premium coaching, analytics, smart generation, Apple Health integration, and Apple Watch features. Manual workout logging remains free. Eligible users may restore Premium Unlock through Apple.

Accounts and collaboration

SuggestMeSome v1 does not create a production cloud account and does not include Sign in with Apple. Coach collaboration, private sharing, invites, coach notes, assignments, visibility scopes, and push notifications are not available in this v1 App Store release.

Use the local export, local backup, and local delete controls for data stored on this device. Contact privacy@suggestmesome.app for privacy questions.

Apple Health, revocation, and deletion

Apple Health access is optional and user-controlled. Deleting local app data does not delete records already stored in Apple Health.

You may delete local app data from the app. Local deletion does not automatically delete Apple Health records, local files you exported, or messages you sent to support. Since v1 has no production account or collaboration service, there is no backend account, coach relationship, private share, or APNs backend registration to revoke.
