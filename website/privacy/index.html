Privacy Policy

How SuggestMeSome v1 handles local workout data, Premium Unlock, optional Apple Health access, and privacy choices in the United States.

Version

Version 2.3. Effective 2026-05-02.

Support: support@suggestmesome.app. Privacy: privacy@suggestmesome.app.

Apple Health-derived recovery data stays on device in this release and is not sent to CloudKit or a sync backend.

SuggestMeSome does not use Apple Health data for advertising and does not include ads or third-party analytics in this public launch baseline.

Premium Unlock is a one-time in-app purchase. Manual workout logging remains free.

Production cloud accounts, Sign in with Apple, backend sync, coach collaboration, private sharing, and push notifications are not included in this v1 App Store release.

Overview

SuggestMeSome is published by Alexander Yao. It is a fitness and wellness app, not a medical or emergency service.

  • Support: support@suggestmesome.app
  • Privacy: privacy@suggestmesome.app
  • Support Center: suggestmesome.app/support
  • Privacy Choices: suggestmesome.app/privacy-choices

Data categories

  • Workout logs, exercise entries, sets, notes, and workout timing
  • Exercise library data and personal records
  • Readiness check-ins, coaching outputs, trend summaries, and recovery estimates
  • Optional Apple Health data you authorize, including sleep, resting heart rate, heart rate variability, active energy, step count, body mass, and workouts
  • Device-side Premium Unlock entitlement state
  • Local backup or export files you choose to create
  • Support or privacy emails you choose to send

How data is used

  • Provide workout logging, history, export, and deletion tools
  • Generate smart workout, program, and coaching guidance
  • Calculate trend, readiness, recovery, and progress summaries
  • Support optional Apple Health read and write flows that you request
  • Validate Premium Unlock on your device and restore eligible purchases through Apple
  • Respond to support or privacy messages you send

Local storage

This v1 App Store release stores workout, coaching, analytics, Apple Health-derived recovery summaries, and Premium Unlock entitlement state locally on this device.

Production cloud accounts, Sign in with Apple, backend sync, coach collaboration, private sharing, and push notifications are not included in this release.

Sharing and disclosures

SuggestMeSome does not use Apple Health data for advertising and does not include ads or third-party analytics in this public launch baseline.

  • Apple handles in-app purchases and purchase restoration.
  • Apple Health is accessed only when you grant permission.
  • Support or privacy emails are sent only when you choose to contact us.

Retention, deletion, and controls

Local workout and coaching data remain on your device until you delete them. SuggestMeSome v1 does not retain production backend account, sync, collaboration, or push-notification records.

Use the app or the privacy choices page to learn about local export, local backup, and local deletion options. Deleting local app data does not delete records stored in Apple Health.

For support or privacy questions, email privacy@suggestmesome.app.
