Help topics

  • Premium Unlock: Purchases and restores are handled through Apple. Use Restore Purchases in the app if your prior unlock does not appear on a device.
  • Account & Cloud: Sign in with Apple is optional. Manual workout logging still works locally while signed out.
  • Apple Health: Apple Health access is optional and user-controlled. Deleting local app data does not remove records already stored in Apple Health.
  • Privacy Rights: Use the in-app account and privacy request screens or the privacy choices page for access, export, delete-data, and delete-account requests.
