Support

Need help with Premium Unlock, cloud sync, account deletion, or Apple Health?

  • Email: support@suggestmesome.app
  • Privacy: privacy@suggestmesome.app

Product posture

  • The app itself is free on the App Store.
  • Premium Unlock is a one-time in-app purchase.
  • Apple Health is optional and user-controlled.
  • Apple Health-derived recovery data stays on device in this release.
  • Cloud sync and coach collaboration are optional.
