Privacy Choices

Use these controls to manage cloud sync, privacy requests, collaboration sharing, and local data in SuggestMeSome.

In the app

  • Account & Cloud: Sign in with Apple is optional. Staying signed out keeps the free workout logger local on your device.
  • Access & Export: Submit access and export requests for backend-held account, collaboration, notification-preference, and synced training data.
  • Delete Data / Delete Account: Submit delete-data or delete-account requests for backend-held records, or delete local workout data separately from Settings.
  • Collaboration revocation: Revoke coach relationships, program shares, and progress shares from the collaboration surfaces.
  • Notifications: Turn push notifications on or off in iOS Settings and in the app’s notification preferences.

What stays local

Apple Health-derived recovery data stays on device in this release and is not sent to the sync backend. Deleting local app data does not delete records already stored in Apple Health.

Need help or want to appeal a denied request?

Email privacy@suggestmesome.app or support@suggestmesome.app. If a privacy request is denied, you may appeal through the in-app privacy request flow or by email.
