Privacy Choices

Use these controls to manage local export, local backup, local deletion, optional Apple Health, and privacy questions for SuggestMeSome v1.

Version

Version 2.3. Effective 2026-05-02.

Support: support@suggestmesome.app. Privacy: privacy@suggestmesome.app.

Apple Health-derived recovery data stays on device in this release and is not sent to CloudKit or a sync backend.

SuggestMeSome does not use Apple Health data for advertising and does not include ads or third-party analytics in this public launch baseline.

Premium Unlock is a one-time in-app purchase. Manual workout logging remains free.

Production cloud accounts, Sign in with Apple, backend sync, coach collaboration, private sharing, and push notifications are not included in this v1 App Store release.

In the app

  • Backup & Export: Create a local device backup file or CSV workout export from Settings.
  • Delete Local Data: Delete workouts by date range or delete all local workout data from Settings.
  • Apple Health: Manage Apple Health permissions in Apple Health or iOS Settings.
  • Premium Unlock: Restore purchases or redeem Apple offer codes from the app or App Store.
  • Privacy questions: Email privacy@suggestmesome.app for questions or appeals.

What stays local

Workout logs, coaching outputs, analytics, Apple Health-derived recovery summaries, and Premium Unlock entitlement state stay local in this v1 release. Deleting local app data does not delete records already stored in Apple Health.

Need help or want to appeal a denied request?

Email privacy@suggestmesome.app or support@suggestmesome.app. If you disagree with a privacy response, you may appeal by replying to that email or contacting privacy@suggestmesome.app.
